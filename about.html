About Us

Who we are

18+ Years of Service Excellence

Established in 2002 and based in Muscat, Sultanate of Oman, Safety House LLC Products and Services is one of the leading companies in Oman supplying products and providing services in Oil & Gas, Power, Water, Petrochemical, Metal & Metal Processing and Project Construction sectors.

We consistently deliver on our commitments to our clients and principals, and as such, we have an unsurpassed track record of excellence as equipment suppliers, service providers, company's representatives, and project development specialists.

We carry out our activities in accordance with our Partners & Principals HSE and QA standards and our code of conduct.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Aim is to be the most trusted HSE solution Provider in Oman

Our Vision is to achieve customer satisfaction, by supplying quality and reliable products, with timely execution.
